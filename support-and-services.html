Support and Services for Pharma Sources

Pharma Sources has a long history in the industry, therefore it is familiar with the requirements and wants of its customers. Our goal is to achieve our fulfillment commitment by offering you the service that meets your demands. Our objective is to offer a variety of services to clients in addition to superior merchandise. Whether it be through routine maintenance, calibrations, installs, repairs, or even technical support, we are here to help you get the most and most out of our company. Our primary objective is to achieve client pleasure and happiness through the provision of great service and high-quality products.

  • We handle all services related to calibration and qualifications, including IQ, OQ, and PQ, along with comprehensive documentation for the instruments and equipment.
  • For Thermal, Mechanical, and Calibration instruments or devices with NABL certification, we also offer maintenance and calibration assistance.
  • We include all technical paperwork necessary to comply with GMP/GLP compliance standards with the product.
  • We work extremely hard to deliver the highest caliber of client service.
