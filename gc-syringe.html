GC & HPLC SYRINGES


Pharma Sources is your trusted partner for high-quality Gas Chromatography (GC) and High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) syringes. With a commitment to excellence and precision, we offer a comprehensive range of syringes designed to meet the diverse needs of pharmaceutical laboratories worldwide.

  • GC and HPLC syringes are used to accurately and precisely introduce liquid samples into the chromatographic system for analysis.
  • Syringes are useful tools for performing routine maintenance tasks such as flushing and cleaning chromatography systems.
  • In HPLC, syringes are often used for precisely measuring and mixing mobile phase components to create the desired solvent gradient for the separation of analytes.


Our GC syringes are meticulously crafted to deliver precise and reproducible sample injections in gas chromatography applications. Made from durable materials and featuring ultra-sharp needles and tight-fitting plungers, our GC syringes ensure accurate results with every injection. Designed for use with high-performance liquid chromatography systems, our HPLC syringes are engineered for optimal performance and compatibility. With a variety of volumes and configurations available, our HPLC syringes provide reliable and consistent results, even in the most demanding analytical environments.

Special features

Precision-engineered for accurate and reproducible sample injections. Smooth plunger movement ensures precise control and minimal sample waste. Low dead volume design minimizes sample loss and ensures maximum sample recovery. Removable needle options for easy cleaning and maintenance. Compatible with a wide range of chromatography applications, including standard preparation, sample introduction, and mobile phase mixing.
