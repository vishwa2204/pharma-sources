Bottle top-Dispensers


Bottle top-dispensers are essential laboratory instruments designed for accurate and efficient dispensing of a wide range of liquids. These versatile devices are commonly used in various scientific and research applications, offering precise volume control, ease of use, and reliable performance..

  • Bottle top-dispensers feature adjustable volume settings, allowing users to dispense precise volumes of liquids with ease and accuracy. This enables reproducible results and minimizes waste.
  • Constructed from high-quality materials such as PTFE, ETFE, and borosilicate glass, bottle top-dispensers are resistant to a wide range of chemicals, ensuring compatibility with different types of reagents, acids, bases, and solvents.
  • Some bottle top-dispensers are equipped with safety features prevent accidental spills and contamination, enhancing laboratory safety and efficiency.


Micro pipettes are essential instruments for laboratories because they allow for the accurate and repeatable transfer of small volumes of liquids in a precise and controlled manner. Numerous scientific fields, including molecular biology, biochemistry, pharmacology, and clinical diagnostics, use these high-precision devices. Micro pipettes provide outstanding accuracy, dependability, and versatility for a broad range of scientific applications.
